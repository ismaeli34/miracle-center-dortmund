Welcome to our Church

Welcome to Miracle Center Church International Dortmund, a Spirit-filled community passionate about worship, prayer, and experiencing God’s power. We believe the same God who healed, delivered, and performed miracles in the Bible is still at work today, bringing restoration, healing, and renewed hope through faith and the Holy Spirit.
