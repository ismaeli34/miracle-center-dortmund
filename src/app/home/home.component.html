Our Pastors

Pastor Mr and Mrs Prince Edos

Pastor Prince Edos and his wife came from Nigeria to Dortmund because they believed God called them to start a church. In 2014, they began with prayer and faith, gathering people to worship and learn about Jesus. What started small has grown into a family of believers where people find hope, love, and encouragement in God.
