Rev Pastor Prince Edos

Church Senior Pastor

Rev. Pastor Prince Edos is a passionate Pentecostal minister from Nigeria, known for his powerful preaching, prophetic insights, and commitment to spiritual growth. Since 2014, he has been in Dortmund, Germany, where God called him to establish Miracle Center Church International. His ministry emphasizes prayer, holiness, and the manifestation of the Holy Spirit, bringing hope, encouragement, and transformation to lives locally and internationally.
