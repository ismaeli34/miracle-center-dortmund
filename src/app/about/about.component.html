About Us

Welcome to Miracle Center Church International Dortmund, a vibrant community of believers dedicated to experiencing and sharing the transforming power of God. We are a Spirit-filled church with a passion for worship, prayer, and the manifestation of God’s miracles in everyday life. At Miracle Center, we believe that the same God who healed the sick, set captives free, and performed wonders in the Bible is still working today. Through faith, prayer, and the power of the Holy Spirit, we witness lives restored, bodies healed, and hope renewed.

Our Purpose.

  • To glorify God by creating an environment where His presence is experienced daily.
  • To guide people into a personal, ongoing relationship with Jesus Christ.
  • To lead people into a personal and transformative relationship with Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior.
  • To nurture spiritual growth through prayer, worship, and the teaching of God’s Word.
  • To equip believers to live victorious, Spirit-filled lives empowered by the gifts of the Holy Spirit.
  • To raise up disciples who walk in holiness, faith, and obedience to God’s calling.
  • To serve our community with compassion, showing Christ’s love in practical and powerful ways.
  • To fulfill the Great Commission by spreading the Gospel locally and globally with urgency and passion.

Our Mission.

  • To proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ with love, truth, and power.
  • To provide an atmosphere where people can encounter God through worship, prayer, and the ministry of the Holy Spirit.
  • To bring healing, deliverance, and restoration to individuals and families.
  • To disciple and empower believers to walk in faith and serve with compassion.
  • To reach out to our community and beyond with the message of hope, love, and miracles.
  • To encourage believers to live Spirit-filled lives, manifesting the gifts of the Holy Spirit in everyday situations.
  • To create a church culture where God’s presence is central, and every member is equipped to impact the world for Christ.

Statement of Faith.

  • The Scriptures: The Bible is the inspired and authoritative Word of God, serving as our guide for faith and conduct (2 Timothy 3:15-17; 1 Thessalonians 2:13).
  • The One True God: There is one true God, who eternally exists as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit (Deuteronomy 6:4; Matthew 28:19; Luke 3:22).
  • The Lord Jesus Christ: Jesus is the Son of God, born of a virgin, who lived a sinless life, performed miracles, died on the cross as a substitute for our sins, was physically resurrected, and is now exalted (Matthew 1:23; 1 Corinthians 15:3; Acts 1:9,11; Philippians 2:9-11).
  • The Fall of Man: Humankind was created good but fell through voluntary transgression, resulting in separation from God (Genesis 1:26,27; Romans 5:12-19).
  • The Salvation of Man: Salvation is received through repentance and faith in Jesus Christ, and it is evidenced by a righteous and holy life (Luke 24:47; John 3:3; Romans 8:16; Ephesians 4:24).
  • The Ordinances of the Church: We believe in water baptism and Holy Communion as commanded by the Scriptures (Matthew 28:19; Romans 6:4; 1 Corinthians 11:26).
  • The Baptism in the Holy Spirit: All believers are entitled to the baptism in the Holy Spirit, which is initially evidenced by speaking in tongues and provides power for life and service (Acts 1:4,8; Acts 2:4).
  • The Church and Its Mission: The Church is the body of Christ, commissioned to evangelize the world, worship God, build up believers, and show God's love (Ephesians 1:22,23; Acts 1:8).
  • Divine Healing: Divine healing is an integral part of the gospel, provided for through the atonement (Isaiah 53:4,5; Matthew 8:16,17).
  • The Blessed Hope and Final Judgment: We look forward to the imminent return of Christ, followed by His millennial reign, a final judgment, and a new heavens and a new earth (1 Thessalonians 4:16,17; Revelation 20:11-15; 2 Peter 3:13).
