Your generosity makes a difference

Bring the full tithe into the storehouse, that there may be food in my house. And thereby put me to the test, says the Lord of hosts, if I will not open the windows of heaven for you and pour down for you a blessing until there is no more need. Malachi 3:10

Miracle center church International is supported entirely from the donations of our visitors and members. We do not receive any funds from the German church tax as most churches in this country do. You can support Miracle center church International(MCCI) financially by bringing your tithes and giving your offerings in the following ways:

1. During the Worship Service

Present tithes and offerings during worship.

To receive a German tax statement: Place your money in an envelope with your name and address written clearly on the outside and put it in the collection.

 2. Direct Deposit (Überweisung)

Make a direct deposit to the church account:

  • Receiver: Miracle Center Church International e.V
  • IBAN: DE49 0000 0000 0000 0000 00
  • BIC: COBADEFFXXX
  • Bank: Commerzbank Dortmund

Note: Include your full name and address to receive a German tax statement.

 3. PayPal

Use PayPal to send your contribution. Visit the church website for the link.

 4. US Dollar Contributions

Write your US dollar check and mail it to:

Miracle Center Church International e.V
P.O. Box 44139
Gutenbergstraße 33, Dortmund, Germany

Important: Write “For Miracle Center Church International e.V” on your check.

You will receive a US tax statement from MCCI to the address printed on your check.

If you have any questions, please contact our treasurer at info@mcci.de

Please know that we will gratefully accept your donation as a gift from you and prayerfully consider how to build the work of the Lord.
