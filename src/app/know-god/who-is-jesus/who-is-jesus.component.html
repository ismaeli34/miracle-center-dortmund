Who is Jesus

Jesus is God in human form who lived in Israel 2,000 years ago. He is not just a good teacher or prophet. He claimed to be the Son of God and the Savior of the world.

  • He said, “I and the Father are one” (John 10:30), showing His equality with God.
  • He claimed to exist before Abraham, using God’s name “I AM” (John 8:58).
  • The Bible calls Him God and Savior (Titus 2:13; 2 Peter 1:1), and Thomas called Him “My Lord and my God” (John 20:28).
  • Old Testament prophecies describe Him as Mighty God, Everlasting Father, Prince of Peace (Isaiah 9:6).

Why God Sent Jesus / His Plan for Humanity:

  • Humans sinned and were separated from God (Romans 3:23; Psalm 53:1)..
  • God sent Jesus to save people from sin through His death and resurrection (1 John 2:2; Romans 5:8).
  • Jesus is the only way to God (John 14:6).
  • As fully God and fully man, He could satisfy God’s justice and die for humanity, reconciling people with God (1 Timothy 2:5).
  • Belief in Jesus brings forgiveness, eternal life, and a restored relationship with God (John 3:16).
